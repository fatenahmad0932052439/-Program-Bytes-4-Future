AKF created Bytes4Future in 2020 in partnership with the Run Code School, the Municipality of Sintra and the Portugal Social Innovation Partnership for Impact.
It is co-funded by AKF Portugal, the Portugal Social Innovation Programme (through EU funding), and the Sintra Municipal Council.
Bytes4Future is a 17-week programme that enables socioeconomically marginalised participants aged 18 to 29 to develop their programming, multimedia and English skills, involving relevant companies to provide access to employment.
It aims to break the cycle of social exclusion and poverty and meet the demand for digital skills in Portugal’s labour market.
